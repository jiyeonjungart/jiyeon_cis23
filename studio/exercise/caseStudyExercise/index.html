Snack Mate
Jia Choi
Snack Mate is a project that showcases series of K-Dramas that Jia watched. Jia used to not be the biggest fan of K-Dramas. However, she often felt left out as many of her friends watched K-Dramas and the main topic of the conversation would revolve around the K-Dramas they watched. Therefore, she started to watch as many K-Dramas as soon as possible. The project presents the K-dramas that she has watched through out the years and how she would rank it out of 5. It also contains conversations with her friends that relates to each K-Dramas.
